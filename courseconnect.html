What is online classroom program

The Intervals online classroom program is a next-level one-of-a-kind tutoring
space where your child can explore his ability to study and further enhance it better
without being bogged down by current unfair teaching styles and practices
currently restricting your child's potential. The classroom course is provided for
students from class K - 12, which has long-term subject packages and short-term
single-subject packages focusing on almost all boards.

The benefits of online classroom course

The Classroom very efficiently aims to hold together each and every child's special
and specific strong points and to tackle the weak ones ensuring that the child is
able to perform better. our classes range from KG -12 which means that no matter
what the class we have a very need-based approach to each class and each
probable area of weakness. Our classes mostly focus on sticking to the base and
providing a personalized take on how a child will learn throughout his/her tutoring
experience. with each teacher expertly looking at your child's progress you can
actually see in real time what is happening.
